About EBO Air 2 Plus FamilyBot

Where Intelligence Meets Intimacy. The EBO Air 2 Plus FamilyBot redefines smart companionship, blending cutting-edge AI with heartfelt connection. Designed for families, parents, and pet lovers, EBO brings life, care, and joy to your home — wherever you are.

With its Smart Dynamic Eyes that follow your gaze, Two-Way Audio & Video Calls, and Boundless AI Adventures, EBO isn’t just a device — it’s your interactive friend, home guardian, and communication bridge.

Powered by advanced AI models, EBO can chat naturally, track motion, patrol your home, and record in crystal-clear 3K Ultra HD. Its whole-home mobility, tumbler stability, and intelligent obstacle avoidance ensure smooth, safe movement across any space.

Stay connected anytime with dual-band Wi-Fi, multi-user access, and privacy protection features. Whether you’re checking in on loved ones, playing with your pet, or enjoying AI-powered companionship, EBO Air 2 Plus keeps your world close — even from miles away.
